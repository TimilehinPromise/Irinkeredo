Transport Route

South Gate

  • Campus Shuttle
  • The campus shuttle charges 50naira for movements that take passengers through faculties and lectiure theatres, bank junction being the first bus stop and Enginneering building being the last bus stop, before reverting back to the bus park in South Gate,
  • This operation is from the early hours in the morning till 6pm.
  • From 6pm the campus shuttle diverts it movements from Obakekere(SouthGate) to Obanla moving through the Student Union building to the hostel making a stop Abiola/Jibowu Hostel junction before reverting movement back to the bus park.

  • Taxi
  • The Taxi takes passengers anywhere within the school premises at an agreed rate between the passenger and driver

North Gate

  • Tricycle
  • The Taxi takes passengers anywhere within the school premises at an agreed rate between the passenger and driver
  • The keke moves from Obanla(NorthGate) through anywhere in school cutting across all lecture theatres and building
  • it serves basically a drop service anywhere in school except the limitation of areas around the Senate Building
