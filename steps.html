PHYSICAL STEPS


  • STEP ONE
  • Go to your department's sacretariat to pay for your departmental dues, you will be given a receipt make sure you have photocopies (5) of these receipt
  • Go to to your faculty's secretariat to pay for your faculty dues, you will be given a receipt make photocopies (5) of these receipt
  • Proceed to your Departmental Registration Officer arrange the dues file to print your course form pending out


  • STEP TWO
  • Arrange all your documents into five places (i.e file office files)
  • Affix your passport to each document
  • Proceed to your departmental office. Follow the instrustions you are given there
  • Your course form will have been approved, print 5 copies of it and add it to each of your files
  • All that being done go back to your departmental office. Follow all instrustions given there
  • You are to proceed to your school faculty registration officer. Follow all instrustions given there
  • Go back to your departmental registration officer. Follow all instructions given there


  • STEP THREE
  • Proceed to STUDENT AFFAIRS building opposite the SENATE building beside PHYSICAL PLANNING UNIT, follow the instrustions given there

  • Take note of these things when going to the student affairs
  • Look presentable
  • Be polite
  • Dress well


  • STEP FOUR
  • Go back to your Departmental Registration Officer and do what you are asked to do
